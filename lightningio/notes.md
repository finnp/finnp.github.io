### Job Factors

- **Job Strain**
- Low Decision Latitude
- Low Social Support
- High Psychological Demands
- **Efford-Reward Imbalance**
- High Job Insecurity
- *(Work-Family Imbalance)*

### High prevalence Jobs
* Nurses
* Bus Drivers
* Teachers
* Lawyers
* Real Estate Agents
* Cleaning Staff
* *Industries with frequent interaction with clients*

*Source:  Wulsin et al. 2014*
